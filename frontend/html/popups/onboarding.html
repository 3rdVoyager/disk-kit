Welcome to Disk Kit

Disk Kit helps you browse, organize, and clean up files on your PC with a focused set of tools.

Use Browse Files to navigate folders, view details, and move items to the Recycle Bin.

Pin your favorite tools to Quick Tools from the All Tools page for fast access from the sidebar and dashboard.

You're ready to go. Use the header search to find files, and change your working path anytime with Change Path.
